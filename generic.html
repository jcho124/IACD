What We Do


Our mission is to hand over the past invaluable experience of Korean development through scientific activities. IACD also desires to help the northern countries and the Third World , our neighbors, by sending different types of specialists.

Programs & Services

IACD sends out professional workers in various fields to overseas to aid the social and economical development in the region. At the headquarters, these professionals are trained to meet the specific needs of their respective countries, and are required to complete courses in area studies, and cross-cultural understanding and adaptation.

IACD also has volunteer program where young adults, students, medical doctors ,scholars, and area study team provide community services and perform researches abroad.

Areas of Service

The center provides medical treatment in its clinics, but its other activities include mobile clinics in rural areas, education for mothers on prevention of disease, and dental training program.
Also IACD has been supporting to open the IT Center with newest computer system and installation to prepare the information age.

Medical Service

The institute is providing medical services in overseas to those who do not have access to adequate medical care.
