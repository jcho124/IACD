Introduction

IACD is a pure international non-governmental organization composed of professional specialists. It is a corporation registered in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Republic of Korea in 1993.

Learn more

Purpose

To promote the international reciprocal understanding and relationship in non-governmental way through community specialists and researchers among all Asian countries in various fields such as human knowledge, society, religion, art, culture and so on.

Learn more

Mission

Institute of Asian Culture And Development seeks to bring about greater cooperation between nation-states in order to complement each others strengths as well as weaknesses.
Through humanitarian assistance, allocation of development professionals, and the exchange of successes and failures, we will strive to build a more peaceful global community.
